> Quick and easy taco chicken. I usually eat this over broccoli but it is versatile.
>
> I use the Spice Island taco seasoning from Costco.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
- [ ] 1 can chipotle chilies in adobo sauce
- [ ] Taco seasoning to taste
- [ ] 1 can tomato purée (optional)

## Instructions

1. Place chicken breasts
2. Add cans of chilies and tomatoes (if using)
3. Sprinkle (or dump, I use about 1/3 cup) taco seasoning on top
4. Pressure cook on High for 15 minutes
5. Wait 15 minutes after it is done cooking before releasing the pressure
6. Shred the meat, and the chilies, using two forks
