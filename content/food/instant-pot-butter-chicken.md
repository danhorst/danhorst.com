## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 14-ounce canned tomatoes
- [ ] 5&ndash;6 cloves garlic
- [ ] 1&ndash;2 teaspoons minced ginger
- [ ] 1 teaspoon turmeric
- [ ] &#189; teaspoon cayenne
- [ ] 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- [ ] 1 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1 teaspoon garam masala
- [ ] 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- [ ] 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts (or ~1 pound boneless skinless chicken thiges. PRO TIP: If chicken is frozen, add 1&ndash;2 minutes to cook time)

### Finish

- [ ] 4 ounces butter cut into cubes (use coconut oil if dairy free)
- [ ] 4 ounces heavy cream (use full-fat coconut milk if dairy free)
- [ ] 1 teaspoon garam masala
- [ ] OPTIONAL: &#188;&ndash;&#189; cup chopped cilantro
