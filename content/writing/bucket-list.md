A "bucket list"--a list of things you want to do or places you want to go before you die--is a ubiquitous part of self-help culture in America.
I maintained such a list on this website for a long time.
I stopped.
I no longer find it a helpful exercise.

I've soured a bit on travel.
It's partly fallout from the long tail of the pandemic.
It's partly because the last tendrils of my youth are fading away.
