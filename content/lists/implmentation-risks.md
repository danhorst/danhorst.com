> This list is derived from [_The Four Big Risks_][1].
> These concepts are covered in detail in [_Inspired_][2].
> I've adapted it slightly to better fit an enterprise IT context.

<dl>
<dt>Value risk</dt>
<dd>Whether partners will adopt it and users will choose to use it</dd>
<dt>Usability risk</dt>
<dd>Whether users can figure out how to use it</dd>
<dt>Feasibility risk</dt>
<dd>Whether our engineers can build what we need with the time, skills, and technology we have</dd>
<dt>Business viability risk</dt>
<dd>Whether this solution will integrate into our existing service portfolio, organizational capabilities, and budget to meet this need</dd>
</dl>
