title: "Mary’s scripture"
description: "These are passages from the journal of Mary Bontrager, my grandma, that will be read at her funeral"

## [Aaron][1]: Psalm 23:2

He leads me beside still waters.

## [Dan][2]: Psalm 94:19

When the cares of my heart are many, your consolations cheer my soul.

## [Leah][3]: Proverbs 10:19

When words are many, transgression is not lacking, but the prudent are restrained in speech.

## [David][4]: Isaiah 26:3

Those of steadfast mind you keep in peace because they trust in you.

## [Andrew][5]: Isaiah 60:5

Then you shall see and be radiant; your heart shall thrill and rejoice.

## [Aaron][1]: Isaiah 65:24

Before they call I will answer; while they are yet speaking I will hear.

## [Dan][2]: Matthew 6:4

Your Father who sees in secret will reward you.

## [Leah][3]: Matthew 21:42

Jesus said to them, “Have you never read in the scriptures: ‘The stone that the builders rejected has become the cornerstone; this was the Lord’s doing, and it is amazing in our eyes’?

## [David][4]: 1st Corinthians 16:15

They have devoted themselves to the service of the saints;

## [Andrew][5]: Ephesians 1:18

So that, with the eyes of your heart enlightened, you may know what is the hope to which he has called you, what are the riches of his glorious inheritance among the saints.

## [Aaron][1]: Colossians 3:15

And let the peace of Christ rule in your hearts, to which indeed you were called in the one body.
And be thankful.

## [Dan][2]: Hebrews 2:18

Because he himself was tested by what he suffered, he is able to help those who are being tested.

## [Leah][3]: James 5:7

Be patient, therefore, beloved, until the coming of the Lord.

## [David][4]: James 5:13

Are any among you suffering?
They should pray.
Are any cheerful?
They should sing songs of praise.

## [Andrew][5]: James 5:16

Confess your sins to one another and pray for one another, so that you may be healed.
