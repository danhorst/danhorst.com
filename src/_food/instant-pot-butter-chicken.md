## Ingredients

Place ingredients in Instant Pot with the chicken on top and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Let pressure release naturally for 10 minutes.

- [ ] 1 14-ounce canned tomatoes
- [ ] 5&ndash;6 cloves garlic
- [ ] 1&ndash;2 teaspoons minced ginger
- [ ] 1 teaspoon turmeric
- [ ] &#189; teaspoon cayenne
- [ ] 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- [ ] 1 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1 teaspoon garam masala
- [ ] 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- [ ] 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts (or ~1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs. PRO TIP: If chicken is frozen, add 1&ndash;2 minutes to cook time)

### Finish

Remove chicken, let sauce cool for a while, add the following, and blend with an [immersion blender][3]

- [ ] 4 ounces butter cut into cubes (use coconut oil if dairy free)
- [ ] 4 ounces heavy cream (use full-fat coconut milk if dairy free)
- [ ] 1 teaspoon garam masala

Cube or shred chicken (if desired) and recombine with sauce

OPTIONAL: Garnish with &#188;&ndash;&#189; cup chopped cilantro
