> This list is derived from [_The Four Big Risks_][1].
> These concepts are covered in detail in [_Inspired_][2].
> I've adapted them slightly to better fit an enterprise IT context.

Value risk
: Whether partners will adopt it and users will choose to use it

Usability risk
: Whether users can figure out how to use it

Feasibility risk
: Whether our engineers can build what we need with the time, skills, and technology we have

Business viability risk
: Whether this solution will integrate into our existing service portfolio, organizational capabilities, and budget to meet this need
