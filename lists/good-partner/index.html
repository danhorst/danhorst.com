Express Your Love

  • Know your love languages and those of your partner There are 5 love languages: words, time, gifts, acts, touch. The best way to show you love your partner is to do the things they value most. Make an extra effort if their love languages are different from your own.
  • Tell them you love them every day
  • Show them you love them every day
  • Share emotional labor
  • Remember important dates and make them memorable
  • Take initiative and plan something as a surprise
  • Tell them when something is bothering you
  • Ask them if they want to talk if something is bothering them
  • Be completely loyal to them
  • Be completely devoted to them
  • Be passionate
  • Invest time in building intimacy
  • Put their needs before yours
  • Take care of yourself; it takes energy to maintain a relationship
  • Take walks together
  • Spend time together
  • Hold hands
  • Always offer to help
  • Make small gestures: open doors, carry bags, wash dishes
