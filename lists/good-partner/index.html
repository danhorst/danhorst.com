Be a Good Partner

Build Trust

This list comes from the Gottman Institute. It is also a mnemonic acronym: ATTUNE

  • Awareness
  • Turning toward
  • Tolerance
  • Understanding
  • Non-defensive responding
  • Empathy

Express Your Love

  • Know your love languages and those of your partner There are 5 love languages: words, time, gifts, acts, touch. The best way to convey your love is to focus on the things your partner values. Make an extra effort if their love languages differ widely from your own.
  • Tell them you love them every day
  • Show them you love them every day
  • Share emotional labor
  • Remember important dates and make them memorable
  • Take initiative and plan something together as a surprise
  • Tell them when something is bothering you
  • Ask them if they want to talk if something is bothering them
  • Be completely loyal to them
  • Be completely devoted to them
  • Invest time in building intimacy
  • Take walks together
  • Spend time together
  • Hold hands
  • Always offer to help
  • Open doors & carry bags
